Overview

Mafia Archipelago, Tanzania

Escape to the tranquil shores of Mafia Island, a hidden paradise in the Indian Ocean. Known for its stunning coral reefs, white sandy beaches, and crystal-clear waters, Mafia Island is a perfect destination for relaxation and adventure alike.

The island offers world-class diving and snorkeling experiences, allowing you to explore vibrant coral gardens and swim alongside sea turtles, manta rays, and an array of colorful fish. The unspoiled beauty of the island’s marine life makes it a dream for ocean lovers and adventurers.

We offer tailored tours to ensure you enjoy the best of Mafia Island. Whether you want to dive, relax on the beach, or explore the island’s rich culture, our expert guides will make sure your trip is unforgettable.

Things To Do

  • Diving & Snorkeling: Explore Mafia Island’s vibrant coral reefs, home to a dazzling array of marine life. Dive sites like Chole Bay and Kinasi Pass are perfect for underwater adventures.

  • Beach Relaxation: Unwind on Mafia Island’s pristine beaches, such as Utende Beach and Ras Kisimani. Enjoy the soft white sand and crystal-clear waters.

  • Dhow Cruises: Sail on a traditional wooden dhow and enjoy a magical sunset over the Indian Ocean, complete with fresh seafood and live music.

  • Spa & Wellness: Indulge in rejuvenating spa treatments at luxury beach resorts, offering massages, yoga sessions, and holistic therapies.

  • Seafood Dining: Enjoy fresh seafood at local restaurants, where you can savor the catch of the day prepared with traditional Swahili flavors.

  • Cultural Visits: Explore traditional villages and learn about the island’s history, culture, and daily life. Participate in cooking classes and traditional dance performances.

  • Deep-Sea Fishing: Embark on a deep-sea fishing adventure and try your luck at catching marlin, tuna, and barracuda.

  • Photography Tours: Capture the beauty of Mafia Island’s landscapes, beaches, and vibrant marine life with guided photography tours.

How To Get There

  • Flights to Mafia Island: The easiest way to reach Mafia Island is by flying into Mafia Island Airport (MFA). Direct flights are available from Dar es Salaam and Zanzibar.

  • Ferry from Dar es Salaam: Take a scenic ferry ride from Dar es Salaam to Mafia Island. The journey takes about 4-5 hours and offers stunning views of the Indian Ocean.

Where To Stay

Best Time to Visit

    The best time to visit Mafia Island is during the dry seasons (June to October and December to February), when the weather is sunny and perfect for beach activities. The green season (March to May) offers lush landscapes but comes with occasional rain.

  • June – October:

    The first dry season offers clear skies, warm temperatures, and ideal conditions for snorkeling and diving.

  • December – February:

    The second dry season is perfect for beach holidays and cultural tours.

  • March – May:

    The green season is less crowded and offers lush landscapes, but with occasional rain showers.
