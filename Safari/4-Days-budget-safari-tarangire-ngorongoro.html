Trip Itinerary

Trip Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival at Kilimanjaro Airport

Your African adventure begins the moment you land at Kilimanjaro International Airport (JRO). After clearing immigration, you'll be warmly welcomed by your safari guide who will transfer you to your hotel in Arusha (about a 1-hour drive).

This evening, you can relax at your lodge, meet fellow travelers, and prepare for your safari adventure starting tomorrow. Our representative will brief you about the exciting days ahead.

Accommodation Options

Budget: Ahadi Lodge - Clean, comfortable rooms with hot showers and WiFi.
Upgrade: Arusha Planet Lodge - Swimming pool and restaurant.
Pro Tip: Try to arrive before 3 PM to have time to relax. Local SIM cards are available at the airport.
Day 2

Tarangire National Park

After an early breakfast, we depart for Tarangire National Park (2-hour drive). Known as the "Elephant Playground", Tarangire boasts the highest concentration of elephants in Tanzania and its iconic baobab trees dotting the landscape.

Morning game drive reveals elephants, giraffes, zebras, and with luck, lions stalking their prey. We enjoy a picnic lunch at a scenic viewpoint before continuing our safari in the afternoon.

In the late afternoon, we drive to our lodge in Karatu (about 2 hours), where we relax and prepare for the rest of the day's activities.

Accommodation Options

Budget: Country Lodge Karatu - Affordable rooms with hot showers.
Upgrade: Ang'ata Tarangire Camp - Luxury tents in the wilderness.
Today's Wildlife Highlights:
  • Elephant herds (up to 300 together!)
  • Ancient baobab trees (some over 1,000 years old)
  • Tree-climbing lions (if we're lucky)
  • Over 550 bird species
Day 3

Ngorongoro Crater

Today we explore the Ngorongoro Crater, often called "Africa's Eden" and the "8th Wonder of the Natural World". After an early breakfast, we descend 600m into the crater floor for a full day of wildlife viewing.

The crater‚Äôs permanent water sources support about 25,000 large animals including the rare black rhino. We'll enjoy a picnic lunch by the hippo pool before continuing our search for the Big Five.

In the late afternoon, we ascend the crater and drive to our lodge (1 hour) where you can relax with stunning views of the crater rim.

Accommodation Options

Budget: Ngorongoro Kuhama Camp ‚Äì Comfortable tents with crater views, perfect for sunset photography.
Upgrade: Ngorongoro Lion's Paw - Panoramic views from the crater rim
Did You Know?

The Ngorongoro Crater is the world‚Äôs largest intact volcanic caldera, measuring 19km across and 600m deep!

Activities

Our carefully curated activities maximize your wildlife viewing while offering authentic Tanzanian experiences. All activities are led by expert guides with extensive knowledge of the ecosystems.

Included Activities

  • Daily Game Drives :

    Daily morning and afternoon game drives in custom 4x4 vehicles with pop-up roofs for optimal viewing. Our guides know the best spots for wildlife sightings at different times of day.

  • Photography Sessions :

    Guided photography opportunities with tips from our driver-guides who know animal behavior and optimal lighting conditions. Vehicle positioning for the best angles.

  • Bush Picnics :

    Scenic picnic lunches in the parks at designated areas with toilet facilities. Enjoy meals surrounded by nature with possible wildlife sightings during your break.

Optional Activities

  • Hot Air Balloon Safari ($150/p)

    Magnificent sunrise flight over Tarangire or Serengeti (when extended) with champagne breakfast. Available on select mornings with pre-booking required.

  • Maasai Village Visit ($40/p)

    Authentic cultural experience visiting a Maasai boma (village) near Ngorongoro. Learn about traditions, see dances, and support local communities.

  • Night Game Drive ($120)

    Special after-dark safari in Tarangire (where permitted) to spot nocturnal animals like leopards, genets, and nightjars using spotlights.
