Trip Itinerary

Trip Itinerary

Day 1

Arusha to Serengeti National Park

After an early breakfast in Arusha, you'll depart for Serengeti National Park via the scenic Ngorongoro highlands (approximately 7-hour drive including stops). Along the way, you'll pass villages and enjoy views of the Great Rift Valley.

Upon arrival in the Serengeti, enjoy an en-route game drive through this vast savannah. Depending on the season, you may witness the Great Migration or spot predators like lions, leopards, and cheetahs. Arrive at your camp just before sunset.

Accommodation Options

Budget: Serengeti Safari Haven – Stay in comfort with modern facilities, delicious dining, and incredible sunset views right from your camp.
Pro Tip: Keep your camera ready at all times — wildlife can appear at any moment in the Serengeti.
Day 2

Full Day in Serengeti

Rise early for a sunrise game drive — one of the best times to spot predators on the move. You’ll explore various regions of the Serengeti based on wildlife movements.

After breakfast, continue your game drives with a packed lunch. The Serengeti is home to elephants, giraffes, zebras, wildebeests, crocodiles, and over 500 bird species. Optionally, book a hot air balloon safari.

Return to your lodge or camp for dinner and an overnight stay under the starry African skies.

Accommodation Options

Budget: Serengeti Safari Haven – Stay in comfort with modern facilities, delicious dining, and incredible sunset views right from your camp.
Today's Wildlife Highlights:
  • The Big Five: Lion, Elephant, Buffalo, Leopard, Rhino (very rare)
  • Cheetahs sprinting across open plains
  • Massive herds of wildebeest and zebras
  • Colorful birds like lilac-breasted rollers
Day 3

Serengeti to Ngorongoro Crater

After breakfast, enjoy a final game drive in Serengeti as you make your way toward the Ngorongoro Conservation Area.

Descend into the Ngorongoro Crater for a half-day game drive. This UNESCO World Heritage site is home to over 25,000 animals including lions, elephants, hyenas, hippos, and rare black rhinos.

After the safari, ascend the crater and drive to your accommodation on the rim or nearby Karatu.

Accommodation Options

Budget: Ngorongoro Kuhama Camp – Comfortable tents with crater views, perfect for sunset photography.
Upgrade: Ngorongoro Lion's Paw Camp
Did You Know?

Ngorongoro Crater is the largest unflooded and unbroken volcanic caldera in the world!

Day 4

Return to Arusha & Departure

After breakfast, begin your drive back to Arusha (approx. 4-5 hours). Depending on your flight schedule, enjoy lunch in town or a visit to a local market.

  • Drop-off at Arusha or Kilimanjaro International Airport
  • Optional day-use hotel in Arusha (for evening flights)
  • Extensions available to Zanzibar, Materuni Falls, or Mount Kilimanjaro
