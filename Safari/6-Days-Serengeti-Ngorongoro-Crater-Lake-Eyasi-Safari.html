Trip Itinerary

Trip Itinerary

Day 1

Arusha to Serengeti National Park

After breakfast at your Arusha hotel, depart for the legendary Serengeti National Park. Drive through the scenic Ngorongoro Conservation Area with an optional stop at Olduvai Gorge, the "Cradle of Mankind." Arrive in time for afternoon game drives across the endless plains, searching for the Big Five and witnessing the incredible wildlife diversity that makes the Serengeti world-famous.

As the sun sets over the savanna, retreat to your luxury tented camp in the Seronera region, the heart of the Serengeti, where you'll enjoy gourmet dining under the African stars.

Accommodation

Mawe Tented Camp – Luxury semi-permanent camp with just 12 tents facing Ngarenanyuki River. Features en-suite bathrooms, private verandas with savannah views, and authentic safari ambiance.

Day 2

Full Day Serengeti Exploration

Enjoy a full day of game drives in Central Serengeti's Seronera region, known for its year-round wildlife concentrations. The network of rivers supports abundant wildlife, making this your best opportunity to spot the "Big Five" (lion, leopard, elephant, buffalo, and rhino). Witness the dramatic predator-prey interactions that make the Serengeti ecosystem so dynamic.

Permanent water sources attract giraffes, impalas, warthogs, and hippos, while cheetahs patrol the open plains. Your expert guide will help you spot and understand the complex behaviors of these magnificent animals.

Accommodation

Mawe Tented Camp – Another night at our luxury tented camp, with evening cocktails around the campfire and the sounds of the African night.

Day 3

Another Day in Serengeti

Another full day to explore the Serengeti's wonders. Morning and afternoon game drives reveal different aspects of this incredible ecosystem. Enjoy picnic lunches in the bush, surrounded by wildlife. Your guide will take you to prime locations for wildlife viewing, whether you're interested in big cats, elephant herds, or the spectacular birdlife.

As the day ends, return to camp for sundowners on your private veranda, followed by a farewell dinner celebrating your Serengeti adventures.

Accommodation

Mawe Tented Camp – Final night in the Serengeti with special bush dinner under the stars.

Day 4

Serengeti to Ngorongoro Crater

After breakfast, depart for Ngorongoro Crater with picnic lunches packed. Descend into the crater floor for a full day of game viewing in this natural wonder. Often called "Africa's Eden," the crater supports over 25,000 large mammals including endangered black rhinos, zebras, wildebeest, and predators. The concentrated wildlife allows for incredible close encounters and photographic opportunities.

In the late afternoon, ascend the crater rim and drive to Mto wa Mbu for overnight stay at a comfortable lodge in this vibrant village known for its cultural diversity.

Accommodation

Marera Valley Lodge – Budget-friendly lodge with clean rooms, en-suite bathrooms, and lush gardens. Perfect base to experience local culture.

Day 5

Ngorongoro to Lake Eyasi

Early morning return to Ngorongoro Crater for additional game viewing, focusing on species you may have missed yesterday. After lunch, depart for Lake Eyasi, a seasonal salt lake at the base of the Rift Valley escarpment. Arrive in time for sunset views over the lake, then prepare for tomorrow's cultural experience.

This evening offers a chance to relax at our lakeside lodge and reflect on your wildlife experiences before tomorrow's cultural immersion.

Accommodation

Lake Eyasi Safari Lodge – Comfortable chalet-style rooms with thatched roofs, private verandas, and stunning lake views. Features a swimming pool and cultural museum.

Day 6

Lake Eyasi Cultural Experience to Arusha

Before breakfast, visit the Hadzabe people, one of Africa's last remaining hunter-gatherer tribes. Participate in their morning activities, learning ancient survival skills and hearing their unique click language. Later, visit the Datoga (Mang'ati) tribe, skilled blacksmiths and pastoralists.

After these unforgettable cultural encounters, enjoy breakfast and lunch before returning to Arusha, arriving by late afternoon with memories to last a lifetime.
