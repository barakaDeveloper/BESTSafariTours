Safari Description

Embark on a 6-day Tanzania safari that takes you through some of the country’s most iconic destinations. This adventure includes visits to Tarangire National Park, known for its massive elephant herds; Lake Manyara National Park, famous for its tree-climbing lions; the breathtaking Ngorongoro Crater, a UNESCO World Heritage Site; and Lake Eyasi, home to the Hadzabe and Datoga tribes. This tour offers a perfect blend of wildlife, culture, and stunning landscapes.

What to Expect on This Safari

Explore Tarangire’s Elephant Herds: Witness large herds of elephants, baobab trees, and diverse wildlife in Tarangire National Park.

Discover Lake Manyara’s Unique Wildlife: Spot tree-climbing lions, flamingos, and a variety of bird species in this scenic park.

Experience the Ngorongoro Crater: Descend into the world’s largest intact volcanic caldera, teeming with wildlife, including the rare black rhino.

Cultural Immersion at Lake Eyasi: Interact with the Hadzabe and Datoga tribes, learning about their ancient hunting and gathering traditions.

Trip Itinerary

Trip Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival in Arusha / Kilimanjaro Airport

Upon your arrival at Kilimanjaro Airport, our professional and friendly team will warmly welcome you. You'll be transferred to your hotel in Arusha, where you can relax and prepare for the adventure ahead. Enjoy a comfortable evening and a good night's rest, ready to start your safari the next day.

Accommodation

Arusha Planet Lodge – A mid-range lodge with excellent facilities, including a restaurant, bar, and swimming pool.

Day 2

Tarangire National Park

After breakfast, depart for Tarangire National Park, famous for its massive elephant herds and iconic baobab trees. Enjoy a full day of game drives, spotting lions, zebras, giraffes, and wildebeests. The Tarangire River attracts a variety of wildlife, making it a hotspot for game viewing. A picnic lunch in the park allows you to soak in the serene surroundings.

Accommodation

Tarangire Sopa Lodge – A mid-range lodge offering comfortable rooms, a swimming pool, and stunning views of the park.

Day 3

Lake Manyara National Park

Today, you'll explore Lake Manyara National Park, known for its tree-climbing lions and diverse birdlife. The park's lush groundwater forests, open grasslands, and alkaline lake provide a stunning backdrop for game drives. Spot elephants, hippos, flamingos, and more as you explore this unique ecosystem.

Accommodation

Lake Manyara tortills Camp – A mid-range tented camp with panoramic views of the lake and surrounding landscapes.

Day 4

Ngorongoro Crater

Descend into the Ngorongoro Crater, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the most wildlife-rich areas in Africa. The crater is home to the Big Five, including the rare black rhino. Enjoy a full day of game drives, with a picnic lunch by a scenic lake. The crater’s diverse landscapes and abundant wildlife make it a highlight of your safari.

Accommodation

Ngorongoro Ang'ata Luxury Tented Camp – A luxury lodge offering breathtaking views of the crater and exceptional service.

Day 5

Lake Eyasi Cultural Experience

Today, you'll visit Lake Eyasi, home to the Hadzabe and Datoga tribes. Interact with the Hadzabe, one of the last hunter-gatherer tribes in Africa, and learn about their ancient traditions. Participate in activities like traditional hunting, bead-making, and cultural dances. This immersive experience offers a unique insight into Tanzania’s rich cultural heritage.

Accommodation

Lake Eyasi Lodge – A comfortable lodge offering stunning views of the lake and surrounding landscapes.

Day 6

Departure

After breakfast, you'll be transferred to Kilimanjaro Airport for your departure. Reflect on the incredible memories of your safari as you bid farewell to Tanzania.

Activities

Our carefully curated activities maximize your wildlife viewing while offering authentic Tanzanian experiences. All activities are led by expert guides with extensive knowledge of the ecosystems. We offer a mix of included and optional adventures to customize your safari.

Included Activities

  • Daily Game Drives

    Morning and afternoon wildlife viewing in custom 4x4 vehicles with pop-up roofs. Our expert guides take you to prime locations to observe the Big Five and Great Migration (seasonal).

  • Crater Exploration

    Full-day Ngorongoro Crater descent to witness one of Africa's most concentrated wildlife areas, including rare black rhinos. Includes picnic lunch at scenic locations.

  • Birdwatching

    Guided birdwatching focusing on Tanzania's 1,100+ species, particularly in Tarangire's swamps and Ngorongoro's lakes. Binoculars provided.

Optional Activities

  • Hot Air Balloon Safari ($150/p)

    Sunrise balloon flight over Serengeti with 360° wildlife views, concluding with champagne breakfast in the bush. Available June-February.

  • Maasai Village Visit ($40/p)

    Authentic cultural experience including traditional dances, homestead tour, and craft market. Directly supports local community.

  • Hadzabe Tribe Experience ($90/p)

    Visit Lake Eyasi to interact with the Hadzabe, one of Africa's last hunter-gatherer tribes. Participate in traditional activities and learn about their ancient way of life.

  • Night Game Drive ($120/p)

    After-dark safari using spotlights to spot nocturnal species like leopards and genets. Specialist guide included.
