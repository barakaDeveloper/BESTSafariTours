Overview

Mwanza, Tanzania

Discover the hidden gem of Saanane Island, a peaceful island in Lake Victoria that offers a unique blend of wildlife, tranquility, and adventure. Perfect for a short getaway, Saanane is Tanzania’s first national park on an island.

Here, you can spot animals like impalas, zebras, and vervet monkeys, all while enjoying breathtaking views of the lake. It’s a great destination for birdwatchers too, with many species nesting on the island.

For us, your safety and comfort are our priority. With expert guides, well-maintained facilities, and premium accommodations, we ensure your Saanane Island experience is unforgettable. Whether you’re a wildlife enthusiast, a photographer, or simply seeking tranquility, Saanane Island has something for everyone.

Things To Do

  • Wildlife Spotting: Spot animals like impalas, zebras, and vervet monkeys in their natural habitat. The island’s small size makes wildlife encounters frequent and exciting.

  • Bird Watching: With over 60 bird species, Saanane Island is a birdwatcher’s paradise. Spot species like the African fish eagle, herons, and kingfishers.

  • Photography: Capture stunning shots of the island’s wildlife, scenic landscapes, and Lake Victoria’s serene waters.

  • Nature Walks: Explore the island’s trails on foot, enjoying the peaceful surroundings and spotting wildlife along the way.

  • Boat Rides: Take a boat ride around the island to enjoy panoramic views of Lake Victoria and spot aquatic birds.

  • Camping: Spend a night under the stars on the island, surrounded by the sounds of nature and the lake.

  • Cultural Visits: Visit nearby fishing villages to learn about local traditions, customs, and daily life. Participate in cultural activities and enjoy traditional dances.

  • Fishing: Try your hand at fishing in Lake Victoria, a popular activity among locals and visitors alike.

How To Get There

  • Flights to Mwanza: The easiest way to reach Saanane Island is by flying into Mwanza Airport, followed by a short boat transfer to the island.

  • Boat Transfers: For a scenic journey, travel to Saanane Island by boat from Mwanza, a 15-20 minute ride across Lake Victoria. Our professional guides will ensure a comfortable and informative trip.

Best Time to Visit

    The best time to visit Saanane Island is during the dry season (June to October), when the weather is pleasant, and wildlife sightings are frequent. The green season (November to May) offers lush landscapes and is perfect for birdwatching and enjoying the island’s serene beauty.

  • June – October:

    The dry season is ideal for wildlife spotting and outdoor activities, with clear skies and comfortable temperatures.

  • November – May:

    The green season transforms the island into a lush paradise, perfect for nature lovers and photographers.
