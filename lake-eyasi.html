Overview

Ngorongoro Region, Tanzania

Experience the unique charm of Lake Eyasi, a seasonal soda lake located at the base of the Great Rift Valley. This off-the-beaten-path destination offers stunning landscapes, rich wildlife, and a chance to immerse yourself in the culture of the indigenous Hadzabe and Datoga tribes.

Lake Eyasi is not only known for its beautiful scenery but also for its excellent birdwatching opportunities, with a variety of bird species inhabiting the area. The lake’s tranquil environment and remote location make it a perfect escape for those seeking a deeper connection with nature and local cultures.

We provide personalized tours that let you explore the wonders of Lake Eyasi. From nature walks to cultural interactions, our expert guides will ensure you have an enriching and memorable adventure. Whether you’re interested in learning about ancient tribal traditions or simply soaking in the serene beauty of the lake, Lake Eyasi has something special to offer.

Things To Do

  • Cultural Experiences: Spend time with the Hadzabe, one of the last hunter-gatherer tribes in Africa. Join them on a traditional hunting expedition, learn how they make fire, and discover their ancient way of life. You can also visit the Datoga tribe, skilled blacksmiths, and learn about their craftsmanship and traditions.

  • Birdwatching: Lake Eyasi is a haven for bird enthusiasts, with over 200 bird species recorded. Spot flamingos, pelicans, storks, and other waterbirds along the lake’s shores.

  • Nature Walks: Explore the scenic landscapes around Lake Eyasi on guided nature walks. Enjoy breathtaking views of the Great Rift Valley and the surrounding hills.

  • Photography: Capture stunning shots of Lake Eyasi’s landscapes, wildlife, and cultural interactions. The lake’s serene beauty and vibrant tribal life provide endless opportunities for photography.

  • Lake Exploration: Take a guided walk along the shores of Lake Eyasi and learn about its unique ecosystem. During the wet season, you can even enjoy a canoe ride on the lake.

  • Camping Adventures: Spend a night under the stars near Lake Eyasi. Enjoy the tranquility of the remote wilderness and wake up to stunning views of the lake and surrounding landscapes.

  • Visit Nearby Attractions: Combine your Lake Eyasi adventure with a visit to the nearby Ngorongoro Conservation Area or Serengeti National Park for a complete Tanzanian safari experience.

How To Get There

  • Road Transfers: Lake Eyasi is accessible by road from Arusha, a 4-5 hour drive through scenic landscapes. Our professional guides will ensure a comfortable and informative journey.

  • Flights to Lake Eyasi: For a quicker option, take a domestic flight from Arusha to Lake Eyasi Airstrip. From there, a short transfer will take you to the lake.

Best Time to Visit

    The best time to visit Lake Eyasi is during the dry season (June to October), when the weather is clear, and wildlife is easier to spot. The green season (November to May) offers lush landscapes but comes with occasional rain.

  • June – October:

    The dry season is perfect for cultural experiences, birdwatching, and nature walks.

  • November – May:

    The green season is ideal for photography and enjoying the lush scenery.
