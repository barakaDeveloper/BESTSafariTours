Overview

Arusha (Starts), Serengeti (End)

Experience the breathtaking Serengeti National Park, home of the Great Migration Safari. Located in northern Tanzania, covering an area of 14,750 square kilometers, this world-renowned park is home to one of nature’s most incredible spectacles—the Great Migration, where millions of wildebeest, zebras, and gazelles embark on their annual journey across the plains in search of greener pastures.

Beyond the migration, the Serengeti offers a diverse range of wildlife, including the Big Five—lion, leopard, elephant, buffalo, and rhinoceros—along with cheetahs, giraffes, and over 500 bird species. From endless savannas to scenic riverine forests, the Serengeti is a photographer's dream and a wildlife enthusiast's paradise.

For us, your safety and comfort are our priority. With expert guides, well-maintained vehicles, and premium accommodations, you’ll enjoy every moment of your adventure in the Serengeti. Whether you prefer camping under the stars or relaxing in luxury lodges, our customized itineraries ensure a seamless experience that leaves you with unforgettable memories.

Things To Do

Here are some of the top activities you can enjoy in Serengeti National Park:

  • Game Drives: Explore the park's diverse ecosystems and spot the Big Five.
  • Hot Air Balloon Safaris: Witness the Serengeti from above at sunrise.
  • Bird Watching: Discover over 500 bird species in their natural habitat.
  • Cultural Tours: Visit local Maasai villages and learn about their traditions.

How To Get There

You can reach Serengeti National Park by flight, road, or guided safari. Here are the main options:

  • Flights: Fly into Kilimanjaro International Airport (JRO) or Arusha Airport (ARK), then take a domestic flight to Seronera Airstrip.
  • Road: Drive from Arusha or Moshi, passing through the Ngorongoro Conservation Area.
  • Guided Safaris: Book a guided 4x4 Jeep safari for a seamless experience.

Where To Stay

Choose from a variety of accommodations in Serengeti National Park:

  • Luxury Lodges: Enjoy world-class amenities and stunning views.
  • Tented Camps: Experience the thrill of camping in the wild.
  • Budget Options: Affordable accommodations for budget travelers.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Serengeti National Park is during the dry season (July to October). During this period:

  • Wildlife Viewing: Animals gather around water sources, making them easier to spot.
  • Great Migration: Witness the dramatic river crossings in the northern Serengeti.
  • Weather: Clear skies and pleasant temperatures make for ideal safari conditions.
