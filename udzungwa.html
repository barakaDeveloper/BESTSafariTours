Overview

Udzungwa National Park, Tanzania

If you're looking for a hiking adventure that combines breathtaking natural beauty and unique wildlife encounters, look no further than Udzungwa Mountain. Located in one of Tanzania’s most diverse national parks, this area is a lush paradise of tropical rainforests, mist-covered peaks, and cascading waterfalls.

Hiking through Udzungwa takes you off the beaten path, where you can discover endemic species like the Udzungwa red colobus monkey and the rare Iringa red colobus. The hike leads to stunning viewpoints, where you can marvel at the rolling hills and valleys below.

For us, your safety and comfort are our priority. With expert guides, well-maintained trails, and premium accommodations, we ensure your Udzungwa hiking adventure is unforgettable. Whether you’re a nature enthusiast, a wildlife lover, or simply seeking tranquility, Udzungwa Mountain has something for everyone.

Things To Do

  • Hiking Adventures: Explore the lush trails of Udzungwa Mountain, ranging from easy walks to challenging hikes. Discover hidden waterfalls, tropical rainforests, and stunning viewpoints.

  • Wildlife Spotting: Spot endemic species like the Udzungwa red colobus monkey, Iringa red colobus, and a variety of bird species. The park is a haven for wildlife enthusiasts.

  • Photography: Capture stunning shots of the park’s landscapes, wildlife, and cascading waterfalls. Udzungwa offers endless opportunities for breathtaking photography.

  • Bird Watching: With over 400 bird species, Udzungwa is a birdwatcher’s paradise. Spot rare species like the Udzungwa forest partridge and the African tailor bird.

  • Camping: Spend a night under the stars in one of Udzungwa’s campsites. Enjoy the sounds of the rainforest and wake up to stunning views.

  • Waterfall Visits: Hike to the stunning Sanje Waterfalls, where you can swim in natural pools and enjoy the serene beauty of the cascading water.

  • Cultural Visits: Visit nearby villages to learn about local traditions, customs, and daily life. Participate in cultural activities and enjoy traditional dances.

  • Nature Walks: Take guided nature walks through the rainforest, learning about the unique flora and fauna of Udzungwa.

How To Get There

  • Flights to Udzungwa: The easiest way to reach Udzungwa National Park is by flying into Dar es Salaam or Morogoro, followed by a scenic drive to the park.

  • Road Transfers: For a scenic journey, travel to Udzungwa by road from Dar es Salaam or Morogoro, a 4-5 hour drive through picturesque landscapes. Our professional guides will ensure a comfortable and informative trip.

Best Time to Visit

    The best time to visit Udzungwa Mountain is during the dry season (June to October), when the trails are more accessible, and wildlife sightings are frequent. The green season (November to May) offers lush landscapes and is perfect for birdwatching and enjoying the rainforest’s beauty.

  • June – October:

    The dry season is ideal for hiking, with clear skies and comfortable temperatures.

  • November – May:

    The green season transforms the park into a lush paradise, perfect for nature lovers and photographers.
