Overview

Arusha, Tanzania

Lake Duluti is a breathtaking crater lake nestled on the outskirts of Arusha, Tanzania. Surrounded by lush forests and teeming with birdlife, this serene destination offers an escape from the bustling city, making it a perfect spot for nature lovers and adventure seekers alike.

A guided nature walk around the lake allows visitors to immerse themselves in the beauty of the dense forest, home to exotic birds, small mammals, and breathtaking scenery. The trail is peaceful and ideal for photographers, birdwatchers, and those looking to reconnect with nature.

For a more immersive experience, canoeing on Lake Duluti is a must-try activity. Glide across the calm, glassy waters in a traditional canoe while enjoying panoramic views of Mount Meru in the distance. The lake is known for its rich biodiversity, and while paddling, you might spot eagles, kingfishers, and even monitor lizards sunbathing along the shores.

Whether you prefer a tranquil nature walk, an adventurous canoeing trip, or simply a quiet moment by the water, Lake Duluti offers a rejuvenating experience for all visitors. The best time to visit is during the dry season, from June to October, when the weather is perfect for outdoor activities.

With its unspoiled beauty, wildlife encounters, and relaxing atmosphere, Lake Duluti is truly a hidden gem of Arusha, providing a memorable half-day retreat into nature.

Things To Do

  • Nature Walks: Embark on a guided nature walk around the lake, exploring the lush forest and spotting exotic birds and small mammals.

  • Canoeing: Glide across the calm waters of Lake Duluti in a traditional canoe, enjoying panoramic views of Mount Meru and spotting wildlife along the shores.

  • Photography: Capture stunning shots of the lake, forest, and wildlife, including eagles, kingfishers, and monitor lizards.

  • Bird Watching: Spot over 130 bird species that inhabit the area, making it a paradise for birdwatchers.

  • Forest Exploration: Learn about the local flora and fauna on guided walks through the dense forest surrounding the lake.

  • Relaxation: Unwind by the lakeside, soaking in the peaceful atmosphere and enjoying the serene beauty of the surroundings.

  • Picnic Lunch: Enjoy a picnic lunch by the lake, surrounded by nature and the soothing sounds of the forest.

  • Group Activities: Bring friends or family and enjoy a day of nature walks, canoeing, and relaxation.

How To Get There

  • Road Transfers: The easiest way to reach Lake Duluti is by road from Arusha. The journey takes approximately 30 minutes, offering scenic views of the Tanzanian countryside.

  • Guided Tours: Join a guided half-day trip from Arusha, which includes transportation, a guide, and activities like nature walks and canoeing. Our professional guides will ensure a comfortable and informative trip.

Best Time to Visit

    The best time to visit Lake Duluti is during the dry season (June to October), when the weather is pleasant, and outdoor activities are at their best. The green season (November to May) offers lush landscapes and is perfect for birdwatching and enjoying the lake’s serene beauty.

  • June – October:

    The dry season is ideal for nature walks, canoeing, and photography, with clear skies and comfortable temperatures.

  • November – May:

    The green season transforms the area into a lush paradise, perfect for nature lovers and photographers.
