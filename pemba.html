Overview

Zanzibar Archipelago, Tanzania

Discover the enchanting beauty of Pemba Island, a hidden gem in the Zanzibar Archipelago. Known for its lush greenery, vibrant coral reefs, and serene beaches, Pemba is perfect for those seeking a peaceful escape. The island's rich history and local culture offer a unique experience, making it an ideal destination for both relaxation and adventure.

Famous for its incredible diving and snorkeling spots, Pemba boasts some of the best marine life in the region. Explore colorful coral gardens and swim alongside tropical fish. On land, you can enjoy spice tours, visit traditional villages, and experience the warm hospitality of the local people.

We provide tailor-made tours that let you experience the best of Pemba. Whether you prefer beach relaxation, diving adventures, or cultural exploration, our knowledgeable guides will ensure you have an unforgettable time on this tropical paradise.

Things To Do

  • Diving & Snorkeling: Explore Pemba’s vibrant coral reefs, home to a dazzling array of marine life. Dive sites like Misali Island and Fundu Lagoon are perfect for underwater adventures.

  • Beach Relaxation: Unwind on Pemba’s pristine beaches, such as Vumawimbi Beach and Ngezi Forest Beach. Enjoy the soft white sand and crystal-clear waters.

  • Dhow Cruises: Sail on a traditional wooden dhow and enjoy a magical sunset over the Indian Ocean, complete with fresh seafood and live music.

  • Spa & Wellness: Indulge in rejuvenating spa treatments at luxury beach resorts, offering massages, yoga sessions, and holistic therapies.

  • Spice Tours: Visit local spice farms to learn about Pemba’s rich spice trade history. Taste fresh cloves, cinnamon, vanilla, and nutmeg.

  • Cultural Visits: Explore traditional villages and learn about the island’s history, culture, and daily life. Participate in cooking classes and traditional dance performances.

  • Deep-Sea Fishing: Embark on a deep-sea fishing adventure and try your luck at catching marlin, tuna, and barracuda.

  • Photography Tours: Capture the beauty of Pemba’s landscapes, beaches, and vibrant culture with guided photography tours.

How To Get There

  • Flights to Pemba: The easiest way to reach Pemba Island is by flying into Pemba Airport (PMA). Direct flights are available from Zanzibar and Dar es Salaam.

  • Ferry from Zanzibar: Take a scenic ferry ride from Zanzibar to Pemba. The journey takes about 2 hours and offers stunning views of the Indian Ocean.

Where To Stay

  • Beach Resorts: Stay in luxurious beachfront resorts offering stunning ocean views and world-class amenities. Popular options include:

    • Fundu Lagoon: A luxury resort with private villas and a serene atmosphere.
    • Manta Resort: A boutique resort located on the pristine beaches of Pemba.

  • Private Villas: Enjoy privacy and exclusivity in a private villa. Options include:

    • Pemba Paradise Beach Resort: A collection of private villas with infinity pools and ocean views.
    • Emerald Bay Resort: A luxury resort offering spacious villas and direct beach access.

Best Time to Visit

    The best time to visit Pemba Island is during the dry seasons (June to October and December to February), when the weather is sunny and perfect for beach activities. The green season (March to May) offers lush landscapes but comes with occasional rain.

  • June – October:

    The first dry season offers clear skies, warm temperatures, and ideal conditions for snorkeling and diving.

  • December – February:

    The second dry season is perfect for beach holidays and cultural tours.

  • March – May:

    The green season is less crowded and offers lush landscapes, but with occasional rain showers.
