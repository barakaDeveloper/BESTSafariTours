Overview

Arusha (Starts), Lake Natron (End)

Experience the unique beauty of Lake Natron, a stunning alkaline lake located at the base of the impressive Ol Doinyo Lengai volcano. Known for its striking red and orange hues, caused by salt-loving microorganisms, Lake Natron is a breathtaking sight, especially at sunrise and sunset.

This remote destination is famous for its large populations of lesser flamingos, which flock to the lake to breed. Witnessing these beautiful birds against the backdrop of the vibrant lake and towering volcanoes is a photographer's dream. The area also offers a chance to explore fascinating landscapes, including soda deposits and geothermal springs.

From guided hikes to the nearby volcano to relaxing by the lake's edge while watching flamingos, our experienced guides will ensure you get the most out of your adventure. Whether you prefer a comfortable lodge or a camping experience under the stars, your trip to Lake Natron will be unforgettable.

Things To Do

  • Flamingo Watching: Witness thousands of lesser flamingos feeding and breeding in the alkaline waters of Lake Natron. The sight of these pink birds against the vibrant lake is truly mesmerizing.

  • Hike Ol Doinyo Lengai: Embark on a guided hike to the summit of Ol Doinyo Lengai, an active volcano known as the "Mountain of God." Enjoy breathtaking views of the surrounding landscapes.

  • Photography Safaris: Capture stunning shots of Lake Natron’s unique landscapes, flamingos, and volcanic views. The lake’s vibrant colors and dramatic scenery provide endless opportunities for photography.

  • Explore Geothermal Springs: Visit the nearby geothermal springs and soda deposits, where you can soak in the warm waters and marvel at the unique geological formations.

  • Cultural Visits: Meet the local Maasai communities and learn about their traditions, customs, and way of life. Participate in cultural activities and enjoy traditional dances.

  • Camping Adventures: Spend a night under the stars near Lake Natron. Enjoy the tranquility of the remote wilderness and wake up to stunning views of the lake and volcanoes.

  • Sunrise and Sunset Views: Experience the magical colors of Lake Natron at sunrise and sunset, when the lake’s hues are most vibrant.

  • Visit Engare Sero Waterfalls: Take a guided hike to the Engare Sero Waterfalls, where you can swim in the cool pools and enjoy the scenic beauty of the area.

How To Get There

  • Road Transfers: Lake Natron is accessible by road from Arusha, a 4-5 hour drive through scenic landscapes. Our professional guides will ensure a comfortable and informative journey.

  • Flights to Lake Natron: For a quicker option, take a domestic flight from Arusha to Lake Natron Airstrip. From there, a short transfer will take you to the lake.

Best Time to Visit

    The best time to visit Lake Natron is during the dry season (June to October), when the weather is clear, and the flamingos are most active. The green season (November to May) offers lush landscapes but comes with occasional rain.

  • June – October:

    The dry season is perfect for flamingo watching, hiking, and photography.

  • November – May:

    The green season is ideal for birdwatching and enjoying the lush scenery.
