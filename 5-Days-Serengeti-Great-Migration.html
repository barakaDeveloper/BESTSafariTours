Safari Description

Embark on a thrilling 5-day Serengeti safari and experience the great wildebeest migration up close. This adventure offers a front-row seat to one of the world’s most spectacular wildlife events as millions of wildebeests, zebras, and gazelles cross the treacherous Mara River, braving crocodile-infested waters. The tour covers iconic areas of the Serengeti National Park, with excellent opportunities to spot predators like lions, leopards, and cheetahs.

What to Expect on the Serengeti Great Migration Safari

Witness the Great Wildebeest Migration: Marvel at vast herds of wildebeests and zebras as they cross the Mara River in search of greener pastures.

Spot Predators in Action: Observe lions, cheetahs, hyenas, and crocodiles taking advantage of the migration—a thrilling wildlife spectacle.

Explore the Endless Plains of the Serengeti: Enjoy game drives across the scenic Serengeti, known for its vast savannah and diverse wildlife.

Birdwatching Delight: With over 550 bird species, the Serengeti is a paradise for bird lovers. Spot species like the lilac-breasted roller, vultures, and secretary birds.

Trip Itinerary

Trip Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival in Arusha / Kilimanjaro AirPort

Upon your arrival at Kilimanjaro AirPort, our professional and friendly team will be there to welcome you warmly. You'll be promptly transferred to your hotel where you can unwind and prepare for the upcoming adventure. After a relaxing evening and a good night's rest, you'll be fully prepared to start your safari tour, embarking on a journey through some of Tanzania's most breathtaking landscapes and wildlife-rich reserves.

Accomodation

Arusha Planet Lodge, Is a Mid-range type in Arusha With Good Condition Accomodation Facilities.

Day 2

Tarangire National Park

After an early breakfast, you'll journey to Tarangire National Park, known for its iconic baobab trees and large elephant herds. This picturesque park offers incredible opportunities to see zebras, giraffes, wildebeests, and predators like lions and cheetahs during thrilling game drives. The Tarangire River, a vital water source, attracts diverse wildlife for remarkable game viewing. Bird lovers can spot over 500 species, including the colorful lilac-breasted roller. Enjoy a scenic picnic lunch in the wilderness while soaking in the serene surroundings. As the day concludes, you'll return to a cozy lodge or campsite for a delicious dinner and restful evening, ready for the next chapter of your safari. Tarangire’s natural beauty and rich wildlife promise an unforgettable experience.

Accomodation

Tarangire Sopa Lodge Mid-range type in Tarangire High Condition Facilities (Bar, lounge, restaurant, TV room, out-door dining, swimming pool, WiFi in public areas). offers Game drives, bush dining, board games, billiards, library.

Day 3

Central Serengeti Adventure

Start your day with an exciting game drive in Central Serengeti, a wildlife paradise full of lions, elephants, and leopards. Explore the endless plains, spot animals like zebras, giraffes, and wildebeests, and enjoy breathtaking views of the famous rock formations (kopjes).

Take a break with a relaxing picnic lunch in the wild, surrounded by nature. Continue your safari in the afternoon, guided by experts who will help you discover the beauty and magic of the Serengeti.

Accommodation

Serengeti Safari Haven – Stay in comfort with modern facilities, delicious dining, and incredible sunset views right from your lodge.

Day 4

Central Serengeti to Ndutu – The Great Migration

After an exhilarating morning game drive in Central Serengeti, where you’ll witness lions, elephants, and leopards in their natural habitat, your journey continues southward to the Ndutu region. This area is the heart of the Great Migration, where thousands of wildebeests, zebras, and gazelles gather in a spectacular display of nature’s cycle. The migration is a year-round phenomenon, but from December to March, Ndutu becomes the calving ground for wildebeests, offering a unique opportunity to witness newborn calves and predator action.

As you arrive in Ndutu, prepare to be amazed by the sheer number of animals grazing on the lush plains. The seasonal lakes and grasslands attract massive herds, making it a hotspot for predators like lions, cheetahs, and hyenas. Your expert guide will take you to the best vantage points to observe this incredible wildlife spectacle, ensuring you don’t miss a moment of the action.

Enjoy a picnic lunch amidst the wild, surrounded by the sights and sounds of the migration. In the afternoon, continue your exploration of Ndutu, capturing unforgettable moments of wildlife interactions and the stunning landscapes of the Serengeti ecosystem.

Accommodation

Ndutu Safari Lodge – Located in the heart of the migration route, this lodge offers comfortable accommodations, delicious meals, and breathtaking views of the plains. Fall asleep to the sounds of the wild and wake up ready for another day of adventure.

Day 5

NgoroNgoro Crater

After an early breakfast, you'll descend into the magnificent Ngorongoro Crater, the world’s largest unbroken volcanic caldera and a UNESCO World Heritage Site. This breathtaking natural wonder is home to an astonishing variety of wildlife, including lions, elephants, buffalos, zebras, and the critically endangered black rhino. The crater’s lush landscapes, ranging from open grasslands to acacia forests, make it a haven for over 25,000 animals. You’ll enjoy thrilling game drives with expert guides, offering incredible opportunities for Big Five sightings. A picnic lunch by one of the crater’s serene lakes, often dotted with flamingos, adds to the day’s charm. By late afternoon, you’ll ascend from the crater floor and head back to your lodge, carrying unforgettable memories of this wildlife-rich paradise.

Accomodation

Ngorongoro Acacia collection acacia collection Known for its stunning sunsets, this lodge provides spacious rooms and easy access to the crater floor.
