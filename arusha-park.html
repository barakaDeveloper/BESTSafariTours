Overview

Arusha (Start & End)

Discover the stunning beauty of Arusha National Park, a hidden gem located at the foot of Mount Meru, just a short drive from Arusha town. This small but diverse park is home to lush forests, sparkling lakes, and vast open grasslands, offering a truly scenic safari experience.

While Arusha National Park is smaller than some of Tanzania’s other parks, it’s packed with wildlife. You can spot giraffes, zebras, buffalos, and even colobus monkeys swinging through the trees. The park is also a haven for birdwatchers, with over 400 bird species calling it home. From the breathtaking views of Mount Meru to the serene beauty of Momella Lakes, Arusha National Park is perfect for those looking for a peaceful but rewarding adventure.

For us, your safety and comfort are our priority. With expert guides, well-maintained vehicles, and premium accommodations, we ensure your Arusha National Park experience is unforgettable. Whether you’re a wildlife enthusiast, a photographer, or simply seeking tranquility, Arusha National Park has something for everyone.

Things To Do

  • Game Drives: Embark on thrilling game drives to spot Arusha National Park’s wildlife, including giraffes, zebras, buffalos, and colobus monkeys. The park’s diverse landscapes make every drive a unique adventure.

  • Bird Watching: With over 400 bird species, Arusha National Park is a birdwatcher’s paradise. Spot species like the flamingo, hammerkop, and Narina trogon.

  • Walking Safaris: Explore the park on foot with expert guides, learning about its flora, fauna, and tracking techniques. Walking safaris offer a closer connection to nature.

  • Photography Safaris: Capture stunning shots of Arusha National Park’s landscapes, wildlife, and birdlife. The park’s scenic beauty provides endless opportunities for photography.

  • Visit Momella Lakes: Explore the serene Momella Lakes, known for their stunning reflections and abundant birdlife. Spot flamingos, herons, and other waterbirds.

  • Mount Meru Trekking: For the adventurous, embark on a trek up Mount Meru, Tanzania’s second-highest peak. Enjoy breathtaking views and a sense of accomplishment.

  • Cultural Visits: Visit nearby villages to learn about local traditions, customs, and daily life. Participate in cultural activities and enjoy traditional dances.

  • Camping Adventures: Spend a night under the stars in one of Arusha National Park’s campsites. Enjoy the sounds of the wilderness and wake up to stunning views.

How To Get There

  • Road Transfers: Arusha National Park is easily accessible by road from Arusha town, a 45-minute drive through scenic landscapes. Our professional guides will ensure a comfortable and informative journey.

  • Flights to Arusha: For international travelers, fly into Kilimanjaro International Airport (JRO) or Arusha Airport (ARK). From there, a short transfer will take you to the park entrance.

Best Time to Visit

    The best time to visit Arusha National Park is during the dry season (June to October), when wildlife congregates around water sources, offering exceptional game viewing opportunities. The green season (November to May) is ideal for birdwatching and enjoying lush landscapes.

  • June – October:

    The dry season is perfect for spotting giraffes, zebras, buffalos, and colobus monkeys.

  • November – May:

    The green season transforms the park into a birdwatcher’s paradise, with migratory birds and vibrant scenery.
