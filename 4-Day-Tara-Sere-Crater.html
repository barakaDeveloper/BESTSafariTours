Safari Description

Experience Tanzania's most iconic parks in this action-packed 4-day safari adventure. This condensed itinerary delivers Tanzania's top highlights with comfortable accommodations and expert guides.

Trip Itinerary

Trip Itinerary

Day 1

Tarangire National Park

After breakfast, depart for Tarangire National Park, where ancient baobabs tower over vast elephant herds. Enjoy full-day game drives spotting lions, zebras, and rare kudus. The Tarangire River attracts incredible wildlife concentrations, offering fantastic photographic opportunities.

As the sun sets, retreat to your comfortable lodge nestled among the baobabs, where you can relax and recount the day's sightings under the African sky.

Accommodation

Marera Valley View Lodge – Mid-range lodge with pool, restaurant, and stunning valley views.

Day 2

Serengeti National Park

Journey to the legendary Serengeti, passing through Ngorongoro's highlands. Arrive in time for lunch followed by afternoon game drives across endless plains. Search for the Big Five and witness the Great Migration (seasonal) - one of nature's greatest spectacles.

Spend the evening at a comfortable tented camp, listening to the sounds of the African night.

Accommodation

Tukaone Weavers Camp – Mid-range tented camp in Central Serengeti with en-suite tents.

Day 3

Ngorongoro Highlands

Morning game drive in Serengeti before traveling to Ngorongoro. En route, visit Olduvai Gorge (optional), nicknamed the “Cradle of Mankind” because it is believed to be the site of found remains of the first human beings to walk on Earth. Arrive at your crater-edge accommodation with time to relax and enjoy breathtaking views.

Accommodation

Ngorongoro Kuhama Camp – Comfortable tents with crater views, perfect for sunset photography.

Day 4

Ngorongoro Crater

Descend 600m into the crater for a full-day safari in this natural wonder. Spot rhinos, lions, and flamingos against the dramatic backdrop. After picnic lunch by a hippo pool, ascend and return to Arusha, arriving by evening.
