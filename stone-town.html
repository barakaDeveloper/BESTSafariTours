Overview

Zanzibar (Start & End)

Immerse yourself in the rich culture and history of Stone Town, the heart of Zanzibar. Wander through narrow streets lined with ancient buildings, vibrant markets, and stunning architecture, reflecting centuries of Swahili, Arab, and European influences.

Explore the famous House of Wonders, visit the bustling Darajani Market, and unwind at Forodhani Gardens as the sun sets over the Indian Ocean.

For us, your safety and comfort are our priority. With expert guides, well-maintained facilities, and premium accommodations, we ensure your Stone Town experience is unforgettable. Whether you’re a history buff, a culture enthusiast, or simply seeking tranquility, Stone Town has something for everyone.

Things To Do

  • Explore the House of Wonders: Visit this iconic landmark, once the tallest building in Zanzibar, and learn about its fascinating history.

  • Shop at Darajani Market: Experience the vibrant atmosphere of this bustling market, where you can find fresh produce, spices, and local crafts.

  • Relax at Forodhani Gardens: Unwind in this beautiful seaside park, especially during the evening when it comes alive with food stalls and entertainment.

  • Photography: Capture stunning shots of Stone Town’s unique architecture, vibrant streets, and scenic waterfront.

  • Walking Tours: Take a guided walking tour through the narrow streets of Stone Town, learning about its rich history and cultural heritage.

  • Cultural Visits: Visit historical sites like the Old Fort, the Palace Museum, and the Anglican Cathedral to learn about Zanzibar’s diverse history.

  • Try Local Cuisine: Sample Zanzibar’s famous dishes, such as biryani, urojo soup, and seafood at local restaurants and street food stalls.

  • Boat Rides: Take a boat ride to nearby islands or enjoy a sunset cruise along the Indian Ocean.

How To Get There

  • Flights to Zanzibar: The easiest way to reach Stone Town is by flying into Zanzibar International Airport, located just a short drive from the town center.

  • Ferry from Dar es Salaam: For a scenic journey, take a ferry from Dar es Salaam to Zanzibar, a 2-hour ride across the Indian Ocean. Our professional guides will ensure a comfortable and informative trip.

Where To Stay

  • Luxury Hotels: Choose from a range of premium accommodations, each offering unique experiences:

    • Park Hyatt Zanzibar: A luxury hotel located in the heart of Stone Town, offering stunning views of the Indian Ocean.
    • Zanzibar Serena Hotel: A boutique hotel with a blend of Swahili and Arabian architecture, offering a serene and immersive experience.

  • Boutique Guesthouses: Stay in charming guesthouses that reflect the unique character of Stone Town, such as:

    • Dhow Palace Hotel: A historic hotel offering a blend of traditional and modern comforts.
    • Emerson Spice: A boutique guesthouse with rooftop views and a focus on local culture.

Best Time to Visit

    The best time to visit Stone Town is during the dry season (June to October), when the weather is pleasant, and outdoor activities are at their best. The green season (November to May) offers lush landscapes and is perfect for enjoying the town’s cultural and historical sites.

  • June – October:

    The dry season is ideal for exploring Stone Town’s streets, markets, and historical landmarks.

  • November – May:

    The green season transforms the town into a lush paradise, perfect for cultural enthusiasts and photographers.
