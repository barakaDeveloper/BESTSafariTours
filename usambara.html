Overview

Lushoto (Starts), Usambara Mountains (End)

Immerse yourself in the cool, misty air of the Usambara Mountains, one of Tanzania's most scenic and biologically diverse regions. Located in northeastern Tanzania, the Usambara Mountains offer stunning views, rich rainforests, and a peaceful retreat away from the bustling safari circuits.

The mountains are perfect for hiking enthusiasts and nature lovers, with trails that take you through ancient forests, tea plantations, and rural villages. The cool climate and breathtaking views over the surrounding valleys make the Usambara Mountains a must-visit for anyone seeking adventure and tranquility in Tanzania.

For us, your safety and comfort are our priority. With expert guides, well-planned itineraries, and premium accommodations, we ensure your Usambara Mountains experience is unforgettable. Whether you’re a seasoned hiker or a first-time adventurer, our customized trekking packages cater to all levels of experience, leaving you with unforgettable memories.

Things To Do

  • Hiking Trails: Explore the numerous hiking trails that wind through the Usambara Mountains. Popular routes include the Irente Viewpoint, Lukozi Village, and the Magamba Rainforest.

  • Explore the Rainforest: Discover the rich biodiversity of the Usambara rainforest, home to a variety of plant and animal species. Guided walks offer insights into the unique flora and fauna.

  • Photography: Capture stunning shots of the Usambara Mountains’ landscapes, forests, and rural villages. The dramatic scenery provides endless opportunities for photography.

  • Cultural Visits: Visit local villages and learn about the traditions and daily life of the Shambaa people. Participate in cultural activities and enjoy traditional dances.

  • Tea Plantation Tours: Explore the lush tea plantations that dot the Usambara Mountains. Learn about the tea-making process and enjoy a fresh cup of locally grown tea.

  • Bird Watching: With over 300 bird species, the Usambara Mountains are a birdwatcher’s paradise. Spot species like the Usambara eagle-owl, African violet, and green-headed oriole.

  • Visit Waterfalls: Take a guided hike to the stunning waterfalls in the Usambara Mountains, such as the Ndelemai Waterfall. Enjoy a refreshing swim in the cool waters.

  • Camping Adventures: Spend a night under the stars in one of the Usambara Mountains’ campsites. Enjoy the sounds of the wilderness and wake up to stunning views.

How To Get There

  • Road Transfers: The Usambara Mountains are accessible by road from Lushoto, a 4-5 hour drive through scenic landscapes. Our professional guides will ensure a comfortable and informative journey.

  • Flights to Lushoto: For a quicker option, take a domestic flight from Dar es Salaam to Lushoto Airstrip. From there, a short transfer will take you to the mountains.

Where To Stay

Best Time to Visit

    The best time to visit the Usambara Mountains is during the dry season (June to October), when the weather is clear, and the trails are in optimal condition. The green season (November to May) offers lush landscapes but comes with occasional rain.

  • June – October:

    The dry season is perfect for hiking, with clear skies and excellent trekking conditions.

  • November – May:

    The green season is ideal for photography and enjoying the lush scenery.
